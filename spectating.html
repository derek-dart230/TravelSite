Spectating


Spectating the Henley Royal Regatta falls into 3 categories. Firstly, there is the general viewing, which is all along the river until
the last half of the race. Next, there is the Regatta Enclosure, where one can buy tickets to watch the race in a covered seated area
complete with shops, food trucks, and gift shops. The highest level of spectating is at the Stewards Enclosure where there is covered seating, dining, the awards tent, rides in the umpires launch during racing, and even viewing all 26 trophies from the start of the regatta.

General Viewing

  • Racing views
  • Open 24 hours
  • Free
  • No dress code

Regatta Enclosure

  • Food and drink
  • Live Announcers
  • Seating
  • No dress code
  • Gift shops

Stewards Enclosure

  • Covered seating
  • Dining and Drinks
  • Formal Dress code
  • View of the finish
  • Trophy viewing
  • Awards ceremony
