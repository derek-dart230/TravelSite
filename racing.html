Racing


Racing at henley is divided into 5 categories, with a total of 26 events in each category.
The categories were made to increase the competitiveness of each event, allowing competitors
from all skill levels to race in a competive environment.

Intermediate

Intermediate events serve
as a bridge between
the club and open
categories, serving as
a good view of
emerging talent

    Events:
  • Men's 8+
  • Men's 4-
  • Men's 4x

Club

Club events allow for athletes
from all clubs to race and
compete at a high level

    Events:
  • Men's 8+
  • Women's 8+
  • Men's 4-
  • Women's 4-

Junior

The junior events are a
way for athletes 18 and
under to compete on an
international stage,
against international teams

    Events:
  • Men's 8+
  • Women's 8+
  • Men's 4x
  • Women's 4x

Student

The student events are
a way for university
students to race on an
international stage, showing
a balance of education and
athleticism

    Events:
  • Men's 8+
  • Women's 8+
  • Men's 4+
